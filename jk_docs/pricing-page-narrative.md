# Workflow Ops — Pricing Page Narrative (Per Plan)

## Free — Visibility Without Risk
**Tagline:** See what’s running. Know when it changes.

Free gives you confidence that your automations exist, run, and haven’t silently drifted.
No governance, no process — just visibility and backup.

**You get**
- Environment connections
- Workflow visibility
- Execution history
- Manual Git backup
- Drift status indicators

**Upgrade when**
You want safety nets and recovery, not just visibility.

---

## Pro — Safety Without Bureaucracy
**Tagline:** Make changes confidently. Undo mistakes instantly.

Pro is for builders who touch production and don’t want fear to slow them down.
No policies. No approvals. Just protection.

**You get**
- Automatic snapshots
- One-click restore
- Manual promotion
- Drift alerts (non-blocking)

**Upgrade when**
You manage multiple environments or other people’s work.

---

## Agency — Governance for Teams & Clients
**Tagline:** Control risk across environments and clients.

Agency is built for teams delivering automations for others.
Repeatability, approvals, and drift control are first-class.

**You get**
- Pipelines & promotions
- Drift incidents & approvals
- Deployment history
- Credential health
- Team roles

**Upgrade when**
You need policy enforcement and audit guarantees.

---

## Enterprise — Infrastructure-Grade Automation Control
**Tagline:** Enforce policy. Prove compliance. Sleep at night.

Enterprise treats Workflow Ops as infrastructure, not a tool.

**You get**
- Drift policies (SLA / TTL)
- Auto-reconciliation
- Audit logs
- Admin & override controls
- Compliance posture

---

## Core Principle
People don’t pay for features. They pay for reduced anxiety.
